Our Story

Quality Service Since 1986

Alan and Mindy King began their journey in Brooklyn, NY, learning the trade from the bottom up before bringing their expertise to St. Thomas in 1986.

What started as a new adventure has grown into the island's premier glass shop. Whether you're talking to Mindy, Alan, or David, you’re getting decades of experience. We treat our customers like guests, helping you make the best of a bad situation with the largest inventory of glass on the islands.
